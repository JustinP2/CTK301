About: "Nirmono" Was Born And Raised on the Eastside of Chicago. Nirmono First Started Off Writing Poetry, As A Way Of Expressing Himself. But In The Summer Of 2019 He Decided To Lay His Lyrical Capabilities Onto A Beat. He Released His First Song Called "Even" On SoundCloud That Did Decent Enough. Though More Importantly It Gave Him The Motivation To Keep Growing As An Artist And To Keep Releasing Music. Nirmono Has Also Worked With And Produced For Many Artist Such as "Premeski", "HoodRixh", "GwuapMan CJ", "Woadie" And More Many. And He has Shown Alot Of Promise With His Latest Album "What Do You Ask Of Me?" Which is Entirely Self-Produced.
